Homemade Pepperoni & Hot Honey Pizza Recipe

Prep Time: 30 minutes (plus 1-2 hours for dough rising)

Cook Time: 15-20 minutes

Ingredients:

For the Pizza Dough (or use store-bought):

  • 1 cup warm water (105-115°F)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar or honey
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (or bread flour)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Pizza:

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (or marinara sauce)
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 4 ounces pepperoni slices
  • Hot honey (to drizzle)
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough (if making from scratch):
    • In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • In a large bowl, whisk together flour and salt.
    • Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour. Stir until a shaggy dough forms.
    • Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, turning to coat. Cover with plastic wrap or a clean kitchen towel and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
    • If using store bought dough, follow the instructions on the package.
  2. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 450°F (232°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
  3. Shape the Dough:
    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface.
    • Roll or stretch the dough into your desired pizza shape (about 12-14 inches).
    • Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet dusted with cornmeal.
  4. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border.
    • Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
    • Arrange the pepperoni slices over the cheese.
    • Optional: Sprinkle red pepper flakes.
  5. Bake the Pizza:
    • Slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
    • Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Add Hot Honey and Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and drizzle generously with hot honey.
    • Optional: Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
    • Slice and serve immediately.

Tips and Variations:

  • For a crispier crust, use a pizza stone or preheat your baking sheet in the oven.
  • Adjust the amount of hot honey to your taste.
  • You can add other toppings, such as mushrooms, onions, or peppers, to customize your pizza.
  • If you do not have hot honey, you can drizzle regular honey, and sprinkle some red pepper flakes on top of the pizza.
  • To make your own hot honey, combine regular honey with a small amount of hot sauce, or red pepper flakes, and heat gently.
  • For a thin crust pizza, roll the dough out thinner, and reduce the cook time slightly.
