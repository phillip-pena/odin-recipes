Simple Ground Beef Taco Recipe

Prep Time: Approximately 15 minutes

Cook Time: Approximately 20 minutes

Ingredients:

For the Meat:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20 or 85/15 is recommended)
  • 1 small onion, diced (optional)
  • 2-3 tablespoons taco seasoning (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup water or beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon oil (olive or vegetable)

For Serving:

  • Taco shells (hard or soft) or tortillas1
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Shredded cheese (Cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a Mexican blend)
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Optional toppings: avocado, jalape√±os, cilantro, lime wedges

Instructions:

Prep the Ingredients (15 minutes):

  1. Dice the onion (if using).
  2. Shred the lettuce.
  3. Dice the tomatoes.
  4. Shred the cheese.
  5. Have all other toppings ready.

Cook the Beef (20 minutes):

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. If using, add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned. Drain any excess grease. (6-8 minutes)
  4. Add the taco seasoning, and stir it to coat the beef.
  5. Add the water or beef broth, and bring to a simmer.
  6. Reduce the heat and simmer for about 5-10 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced and the meat is flavorful.

Warm the Taco Shells/Tortillas:

Follow the package directions to warm your taco shells or tortillas. You can also warm tortillas in a dry skillet or over a gas flame.2

Assemble the Tacos:

Fill each taco shell or tortilla with the seasoned ground beef.

Top with shredded lettuce, shredded cheese, diced tomatoes, sour cream, salsa, and any other desired toppings.

Serve and Enjoy:

Serve immediately with lime wedges, if desired.

Key Points:

  • These times are estimates, and actual cooking times may vary depending on your stove and cookware.
  • Preparing all of the toppings while the meat is cooking, will help to save time.
  • Enjoy!

1 Choose your favorite type of taco shell or tortilla.

2 Be careful when warming tortillas over a gas flame.
