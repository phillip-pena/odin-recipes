Smash Burger Recipe

Yields: 2 servings

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 hamburger buns
  • 2 slices cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • 1 pickle
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

Preparing the buns:

  1. Cut the hamburger buns in half horizontally.
  2. Spread a thin layer of butter on the cut sides of the buns.
  3. Place the buns, buttered side down, on a hot skillet or griddle.
  4. Toast the buns until they are lightly browned and crispy.

Making the smash burger:

  1. Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper.
  3. Form the ground beef into two equal-sized patties.
  4. Place the patties on the hot skillet.
  5. Immediately press down on the patties with a spatula to smash them into thin patties.
  6. Cook the patties for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they are browned and cooked through.
  7. Top each patty with a slice of cheese.
  8. Cook for an additional minute, or until the cheese is melted.
  9. Place the cooked patties on the toasted buns.
  10. Top with ketchup, mustard, and pickle slices.
  11. Enjoy your delicious smash burger!

Tips:

  • For extra flavor, you can add chopped onions or mushrooms to the ground beef before cooking.
  • If you don't have a griddle, you can use a heavy-bottomed skillet.
  • To get a crispy exterior, make sure the skillet is hot before adding the patties.
  • Use a spatula to press down on the patties firmly and evenly.
  • Cook the patties to your desired doneness.
  • Enjoy your smash burger!
